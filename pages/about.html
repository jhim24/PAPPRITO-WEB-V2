ABOUT PAPPRITO

From day one, our vision has always been for our guests to savor the meals they love, at prices they feel good about. Every dish we serve — from Tagaytay specialties such as Tapang Taal, Bulalo and Crispy Tawilis to our festive Pancit Bilaos — showcases our pledge to serve sarap consistently. Our hearty all-day breakfast Rapsilog and hefty Macho and Todo Meals reflect our commitment to provide busog without ripping the wallet. Come visit our warm abode and experience why: "Masarap maging affordable" is not just a tagline, but our guiding principle in serving everyone every day.

A Taste of Home in Every Meal

PAPPRITO Restaurant was established with a simple vision: to celebrate the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine while creating a warm and welcoming place where families, friends, and visitors can enjoy memorable dining experiences.

Every dish is carefully prepared using quality ingredients, authentic recipes, and genuine Filipino hospitality. Our commitment is to serve delicious food that brings comfort, happiness, and lasting memories to every guest.
